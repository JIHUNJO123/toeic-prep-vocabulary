Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my premium purchase?

Go to Settings and tap Restore Purchase button. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account.

Can I use the app offline?

Yes! All vocabulary data is stored locally on your device.

How do I change the translation language?

Go to Settings and select your preferred language from the Translation Language dropdown.

What is the difference between free and premium?

Free users have access to the first level (0-400 score range). Premium unlocks all 3,600+ words.

Is this app affiliated with ETS?

No, this app is not affiliated with or endorsed by ETS. TOEIC is a registered trademark of ETS.
